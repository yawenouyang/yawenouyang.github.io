Bio

I am currently a researcher at Shanghai AI Lab, focusing on llm for science. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Weiying Ma and Prof. Hao Zhou, supported by the NSFC. Before that, I completed my Ph.D. at Nanjing University under the supervision of Prof. Xinyu Dai. During my Ph.D., I also interned at Bytedance AI Lab, where I worked with Prof. Hongning Wang.
